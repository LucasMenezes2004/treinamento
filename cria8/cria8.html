Technology


Technology is a product of science and engineering that involves a set of instruments, methods and techniques aimed at solving problems. It is a practical application of scientific knowledge in several areas of research. In general terms, technology is the application of scientific knowledge to the practical purposes of human life or to changing and manipulating the environment. Techniques, equipment, robots and other things that help in people's routine can be considered technological objects.
